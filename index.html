Imagine. Fantasy Role Playing games, but for VR.

We love Dungeons and Dragons, Pathfinder, and games like these. So we decided to make our visions into your reality. Our VR Dungeon Crawler is based around classic fantasy RPG elements like those found in Pathfinder or Dungeons and Dragon.

Choose your class. Wizard, Paladin, or Rogue.

The Wizard has wicked awesome spells. The Paladin is a knight with magic weapons.The Rogue is like a super awesome ninja. And many more classes to come!

Multiplayer? Hell Yeah.

Were working on a multiplayer so you can play with your friends with or without a VR head set.
