About Us

PotatoFaceGames is the brain child of James Nye and Copeland Williams. They initially started development on an early prototype of VR Dungeon Crawler in April of 2016 with the release of the HTC Vive Virtual Reality Headset. During this time James Nye decided to create his own personal game jam and developed "Space, VR!" in less than a week. Seeing the response to VR titles and the upcoming games market James Nye dropped out of college to start PotatoFaceGames in December of 2016 The next 3 months were devoted to part time development of VR Dungeon Crawler to its current state.
